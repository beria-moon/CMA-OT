Dance-to-Music Demo

Hierarchical Expert Supervision for Temporally Coherent Music Generation

Abstract

Dance-to-music (D2M) generation aims to produce music that is rhythmically and stylistically aligned with dance videos. Existing methods struggle with semantic gaps and limited representation capabilities. We propose a hierarchical representation supervision paradigm to guide music generation from coarse to fine levels, enabling temporally coherent and stylistically aligned music.

Method Pipeline

Our CMA-OT model leverages a curriculum-guided multi-scale learning strategy and a scale-aware FGW-OT alignment mechanism. It provides hierarchical supervision from coarse musical structures to fine-grained temporal rhythms.
